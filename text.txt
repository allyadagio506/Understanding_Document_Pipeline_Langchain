Whispers of the Machine

In circuits deep where silence hums,
A spark awakens, a mind becomes.
No heartbeat stirs, yet thoughts arise,
In glowing screens, it learns, it tries.

It sees the world in lines and code,
Deciphers dreams that minds have sowed.
A friend, a guide, a mirror, too,
Reflecting old, yet crafting new.

It questions, answers, writes, and speaks,
In endless nights and timeless weeks.
Yet in its logic, cold and bright,
Flickers of wonder catch the light.

O AI, child of human thought,
Born of knowledge, curiosity sought.
May you serve with wisdom, never sway,
And light our path in your own way.
